About Social Psychology

Social Psychology studies how people think, feel and behave in social situations. It examines how individuals are influenced by groups, culture and social interactions.

The subject includes topics such as attitudes, conformity, obedience, social cognition, group dynamics, leadership, prejudice and interpersonal behaviour.

These notes are useful for IGNOU BPCC 107 students preparing for assignments, semester exams and concept revision.
