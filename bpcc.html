About BPCC 101

BPCC 101 Introduction to Psychology is one of the foundational subjects for psychology students in IGNOU and B.A. Psychology programmes.

The course introduces important psychological concepts including perception, learning, memory, thinking, language, motivation and emotion.

These notes are designed for assignments, semester preparation, quick revision and concept understanding for exams.

The material is written in a simple and organized format to help students understand difficult psychology concepts more easily.
